Titan's Circuit

How to Play Titan Circuit

• The game consists of concentric hexagonal circuits and nodes of the user's choice.
• Players take turns placing Titans on the outer ring.
• Once a circuit is filled, the next inner ring unlocks.
• You can move Titans or place new ones if allowed.
• Controlling edges between two of your Titans earns points.
• Titans surrounded by the opponent are removed.
• The game ends when either the timer runs out or if the inner circuit is completely filled.
• The player with the highest score at the end wins!
